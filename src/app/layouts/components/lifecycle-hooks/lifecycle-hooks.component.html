Angular Lifecycle Hooks

Angular lifecycle hooks allow developers to tap into component events.

{{name}}
ngOnChanges

Called when an input property changes.

ngOnInit

Called once after the component is initialized.

ngDoCheck

Called during every change detection run.

ngAfterContentInit

Called once after content (ng-content) is projected into the component.

ngAfterContentChecked

Called after every check of projected content.

ngAfterViewInit

Called once after the component's view and child views are initialized.

ngAfterViewChecked

Called after every check of the component's view and child views.

ngOnDestroy

Called just before Angular destroys the component. Used for cleanup like unsubscribing from observables.
